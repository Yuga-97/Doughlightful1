Decadent Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Rich, moist chocolate cake that melts in your mouth. Perfect for special occasions.

by emma’s recipe

by 45 min.
Hard

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round pans.
  2. In large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
  3. In separate bowl, beat eggs, then add buttermilk, coffee, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  5. Divide batter between prepared pans.
  6. Bake 30–35 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool completely before frosting.
